# 🧭 [Lesson 2: God Alone](../index.md)

## ❓ Review Questions

1. Was there ever a time when God was not living?

   _No._

1. What does God need in order to exist?

   _Nothing._

1. Does God have a body?

   _No._

1. How many Gods are there?

   _One God._

1. Who are the three persons who make the one God?

   _God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit._

1. Is there some faraway place you could go on this earth or in this galaxy or in this universe or anywhere that God would not be?

   _No._

1. What do we mean when we say that God is sovereign?

    _He is greater than all and more important than all; He is the highest in authority._
