# 🧩 [Lesson 2: God Alone](../index.md)

## God is ETERNAL, having no beginning and no end

🧵 THEMES - God is greater than all and more important than all; He is the highest in authority.

Before anything came to be, God existed as He does now and will for forever in the future.

God was the only one there at the beginning.

| God | – vs – | man |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| God has no beginning and no end |   | man is born and dies |
