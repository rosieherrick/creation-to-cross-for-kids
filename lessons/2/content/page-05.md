# 🧭 [Lesson 2: God Alone](../README.md)

## 🧩 God is Trinity

|                          | TRINITY |                          |
| :----------------------: | :-----: | :----------------------: |
|      "tri-" = three      |         |       "unit" = one       |
| ![alt text](image-1.png) |         | ![alt text](image-2.png) |

---

We use the word “Trinity” to describe these three persons who are the one Eternal God.

The Word “Trinity” is made from “tri-”, meaning three (as in a tricycle) and “unit”, meaning one – that is, three-in-one.

|                  God                  | – vs – |            man             |
| :-----------------------------------: | :----: | :------------------------: |
|    God has no beginning and no end    |        |    man is born and dies    |
| **God is a Trinity of three persons** |        | **man is only one person** |

---

There is the Father who is God, the Son who is God, and the Holy Spirit who is God.

Hard to understand? Yes, to us it certainly is!

God is so much greater than we can imagine.

- The Trinity is beyond our understanding.
- We can only describe what we understand from God’s Word.

The amazing fact of the Trinity is just one of the things that shows us the **greatness of God, so great that we can't even fully understand Him!**

- 🔎 Consider: _Can you describe gravity? No! Even the scientist don’t really understand it! But we can see what it does. That’s how it is with God. We can see what He does and learn about Him from His Word. But He is far above our ability to fully understand._
