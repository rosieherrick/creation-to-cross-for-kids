# 🧭 [Lesson 2: God Alone](../README.md)

## 🧩 God is ETERNAL, having no beginning and no end

🧵 THEMES - God is greater than all and more important than all; He is the highest in authority.

Before anything came to be, God existed as He does now and will for forever in the future.

God was the only one there at the beginning.

|               God               | – vs – |         man          |
| :-----------------------------: | :----: | :------------------: |
| God has no beginning and no end |        | man is born and dies |

## 📖 READ – Psalm 90:2

_Before the mountains were brought forth, or ever you had formed the earth and the world, from everlasting to
everlasting you are God._

- There never was a time when God did not exist.
- He did not have a beginning.
- He was not created.
- He has always been alive.
- He has always been and will always be the same.
- There never will be a time when God does not exist.
- God can never die – He is ETERNAL – He is forever!
