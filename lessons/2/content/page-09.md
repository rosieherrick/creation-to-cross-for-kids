# 🧭 [Lesson 2: God Alone](../README.md)

## 🧩 God is in all places at the same time

🧵 THEMES - God is greater than all and more important than all; He is the highest in authority.

🧵 THEMES - God is everywhere all the time.

Where is God?

Where was He before everything else existed?

We cannot see God, and we couldn’t know where He is unless He had told us.

- In the Bible, God tells us He is **everywhere all the time!**

- He is the Creator, separate from and greater than all His creation

God is higher than all.

God is greater than all He has made.

- God fills the universe.

## 📖 READ – Jeremiah 23:23, 24

*Am I a God at hand, declares the LORD, and not a God far away? Can a man hide himself in secret
places so that I cannot see him? declares the LORD. Do I not fill heaven and earth? declares the LORD.*
