# 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 [Creation to the Cross - for Kids](../../README.md)

## 🧭 Lesson 3: God and the Angels

### 📖 Scripture – Isaiah 14:12-20, Ezekiel 28:11-18, Romans 8:37-39, Ephesians 6:10-18

**Additional Scriptures for Study:** Jeremiah 32:27, Matthew 16:18, Revelation 1:8

### 🎯 Lesson Goals

- To show the sovereignty and holiness of God in His creation of the angels and in His dealing with Lucifer and the others who rebelled.

### 💡 This Lesson Should Help the Students

- To see that God is greater than all the more powerful than all.
- To see the seriousness of the rebellion against God.

### 🧭 Overview

This lesson establishes that God created all the spirit beings. It presents God who is sovereign and holy, creator than all created beings, creating all things perfect. It also shows God's sovereignty and holiness in dealing with Lucifer's willful rebellion against God.

### ✨🔑 Key Verse – Psalm 90:2

_Before the mountains were brought forth, or ever you had formed the earth and the world, from everlasting to everlasting you are God._

---

### 💭 Perspective for the Teacher

In our society, it has become increasingly popular to glamorize Satan and the demonic
world in movies, music, etc. 2 Corinthians 11:14 says that Satan masquerades as an angel of
light. The Enemy’s subtlety has fooled some (like Eve), while other have simply made knowing
steps of rebellion (like Adam).

Children need to know the truth. God is the sovereign Creator; Satan is a created being
who lost his position as God’s servant. Rather than frighten the children, this truth will give them
stability and understanding. **Throughout the lesson, emphasize God’s greatness and
superiority – His sovereignty.**

As this lesson is studied, you may find that your students have many questions. We need to get our answers and power from the right source, God Himself. Satan is a liar, a thief, a deceiver, an accuser, and a murderer. He wants to deceive as many as possible so they will not listen to God’s Word and believe.

But remember: no matter what opposition we face to the message of the Bible; no matter what difficulties, we have assurance in GOd’s Word that He is stronger than all. Nothing is too hard for Him. God will use His Word in our hearts and will give your wisdom. Our Enemy is strong, but OUR GOD IS STRONGER THAN ALL! In Jesus Christ, we have victory.

### 🗣️ On Teaching This Lesson

You are carefully laying a scriptural foundation on which the Gospel will later be presented. Each lesson builds on previous lessons. So be sure to cover each point carefully. DON’T COMPLICATE THE MESSAGE! Carefully follow the lesson, emphasizing the doctrinal 🧵themes given.
