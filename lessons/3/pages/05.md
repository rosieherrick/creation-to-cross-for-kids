# 🧭 [Lesson 3: God and the Angels](../index.md)

## 🧩 The angels were created with great wisdom and strength

God created the spirits with great wisdom and strength.

## 📖 READ - Psalm 103:20

*"Bless the LORD, O you his angels, you mighty ones who do his word, obeying the voice of his word!"*

🧵 THEMES - God is greater than all and more important than all; He is the highest in authority.

- Angels are stronger than we are, and the Bible tells us that God has given them the ability to do amazing things; but they are not all-powerful like God.
- Angels are very smart, too, but they are not all-knowing like God is.
- God is wiser and stronger than all the angels.
- This truth is very important to remember.
- The Bible tells us the truth that God is greater than all!

## 🧩 The angels are innumerable

🧵 THEMES - God is all-powerful.

God created so many angels that it would be impossible for us to count them.

## 📖 READ - Revelation 5:11

*"...many angels, numbering myriads of myriads and thousands of thousands."*

How could God create so many good, strong, smart angels?

- He is almighty
- He can do anything and everything He wants to do!
