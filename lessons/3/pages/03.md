# 🧭 [Lesson 3: God and the Angels](../index.md)

## 🧩 The angels were created to serve God

🧵 THEMES - God is greater than all and more important than all; He is the highest in authority.

Why did God create the angels?

The angels were all created by God to love and serve Him.

- In the beginning, all the angels were God's angels.
- The word "angels" means "messengers" or "servants"

**GOD'S ANGELS were created to be GOD'S SERVANTS**

- Because God created them, they belonged to Him.
- They were created to do whatever God wanted them to do.

**Show image of child’s art work**

- 🔎 Illustrate: *What if you take your favourite colouring book and your own set of markers or crayons that you have, and you make a **beautiful** picture. Who does the picture belong to? It
belongs to you, doesn’t it. What if you buy a Grand Prix car kit with complete with a wood block, four axels, and four wheels. You work hard to design your car just to your liking and manage to get it all put together. It’s yours', isn’t it? You are the rightful owner of what you have made.*

    *In the same way, everything that God created rightfully belongs to Him. God was the one who made all the angels. He gave them life, He made them to serve and obey Him. Therefore, all of the angels rightfully belong to God.*
