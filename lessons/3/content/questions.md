# 🧭 Lesson 3: God and the Angels

## ❓ Review Questions

1. Who is the only One who lived before the beginning? _God._

1. Where did angels come from? _They were created by God._

1. Did God create the angels with bodies of flesh and bones like us? _No._

1. Did God create good and bad angels? _No, He made them all good._

1. Why did God create angels? _To be His servants._

1. When they were first created, where did all the angels live? _With God in Heaven._

1. Who was the smartest and most beautiful angel God made? _Lucifer._

1. What special job did Lucifer get to have? _He was made leader of all the other angels._

1. What did Lucifer do? _He planned to be like God and take God's position._

1. Who else followed Lucifer in his rebellion against God? _Many of God's angels._

1. Did God know what Lucifer and the rebellious angels were planning? _Yes._

1. What did God do? _He removed Lucifer from his position and kicked Lucifer and the rebellious angels out of Heaven._

1. Is there anything that God doesn't see, hear, and know? _No! God sees, hears, and knows everything._

1. What place did God prepare as a punishment for Lucifer and the rebellious angels? _The Lake of Fire._

1. What is Lucifer's new name that means "enemy", what was that name? _Satan._

1. Who is Satan's enemy? Who is he against? _God._
