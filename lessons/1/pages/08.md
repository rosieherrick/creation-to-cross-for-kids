## 🧩 The Bible was accurately translated into different languages

![alt text](image-13.png)

How did we get the Bible in our language?

- The original books of the Bible were written in either Hebrew, Aramaic, or Greek.
- For many centuries only a few people were able to have copies of the Bible.
- Down through the ages, God enabled men to translate the Bible into different languages.

- Men like John Wycliffe translated the Bible into English for the first time about 750 years ago.
