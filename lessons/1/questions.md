# 🧭 Lesson 1: Introducing the Bible

## ❓ Review Questions

1. God chose men to write down His Words. What are these men called?

   _Prophets._

1. How many years passed before all of God’s Word was written down?

   _It took about 2,000 years before all of God’s words were written._

1. How many men were there whom God used to write down His Word?

   _About forty._

1. Did they write God’s words in English?

   _No. They were written in Hebrew, Greek, and Aramaic._

1. Who is the author of the Bible?

   _God._

1. Why should a person study the Bible?

   _The Bible is God’s personal message to every individual._
